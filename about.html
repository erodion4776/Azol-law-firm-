About CY visa help

Your Trusted Partner in U.S. Immigration & Visa Services

Our Story

Founded with a passion for helping individuals and families achieve their American dreams, CY visa help has been serving the community with dedication and expertise, making the complex U.S. immigration process simple and accessible.

We understand that navigating visa applications and immigration procedures can be overwhelming and stressful. That's why we're committed to providing personalized, compassionate support that puts your needs first and guides you through every step of your journey.

Our service combines deep immigration knowledge with genuine care for our clients, ensuring that every case receives the attention, expertise, and personalized guidance it deserves.

Meet Our Founder

Cynthia Azor, Esq.

Founder & Lead Immigration Specialist

Cynthia Azor is a dedicated immigration professional with over 15 years of experience helping clients navigate the complexities of U.S. immigration and visa processes. She graduated from Columbia Law School and has since devoted her career to assisting individuals, families, and businesses with their immigration needs.

Fluent in English, Spanish, and French, Cynthia has successfully guided clients from around the world through visa applications ranging from family-based petitions to complex business immigration cases. Her commitment to client success and wellbeing has earned her recognition as a leading advocate in the immigration services community.

When she's not helping clients achieve their American dreams, Cynthia volunteers with local immigrant advocacy organizations and mentors aspiring professionals in the immigration field.
