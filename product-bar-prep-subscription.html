Structured Study Plan

Phase 1: Foundation (Weeks 1-4)

  • Diagnostic assessment to identify strengths and weaknesses
  • Black letter law review for all MBE subjects
  • Introduction to essay writing techniques
  • Daily practice questions (25-30 per day)
  • Weekly subject mastery tests

Phase 2: Skill Building (Weeks 5-8)

  • Intensive MBE practice (50+ questions daily)
  • Essay writing workshops (2-3 essays per week)
  • Performance test strategy development
  • Timed practice sessions
  • Memorization techniques for rules

Phase 3: Integration (Weeks 9-10)

  • Full-length practice exams
  • Mixed subject practice
  • Essay speed training
  • Stress management techniques
  • Final review of weak areas

Phase 4: Final Prep (Weeks 11-12)

  • Simulated bar exam conditions
  • Last-minute memorization
  • Confidence building exercises
  • Test day strategy review
  • Mental preparation and wellness
