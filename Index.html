Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the visa application process take?
The timeline varies depending on the type of visa or immigration application. Some processes can take a few months, while others may take several years. We'll provide you with a realistic timeline and keep you updated throughout your visa journey.
Do you help with business immigration and investor visas?
Yes! We provide comprehensive business immigration services including investor visas (EB-5), E-2 treaty visas, L-1 intracompany transfers, and other work-related visas for entrepreneurs and businesses looking to operate in the United States.
What's included in an immigration consultation?
A consultation includes a comprehensive review of your immigration situation, expert advice on visa options, discussion of eligibility requirements, and a clear action plan. You'll receive personalized guidance tailored to your specific immigration goals and circumstances.
