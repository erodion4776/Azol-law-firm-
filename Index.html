Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the immigration process take?
The timeline varies depending on the type of visa or application. Some processes can take a few months, while others may take several years. We'll provide you with a realistic timeline during your consultation.
What documents do I need for my consultation?
Bring any immigration-related documents you have, including passports, visas, previous applications, and correspondence from USCIS. Don't worry if you don't have everything—we'll guide you through what's needed.
Do you offer payment plans?
Yes, we understand that legal services are an investment. We offer flexible payment plans to make our services accessible. Contact us to discuss options that work for your budget.
