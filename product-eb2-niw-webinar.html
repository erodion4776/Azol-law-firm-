Complete EB-2 NIW Training

The EB-2 National Interest Waiver is one of the most sought-after paths to permanent residency for qualified professionals. This comprehensive webinar breaks down the entire process, from eligibility assessment to petition preparation and submission.

Who Should Attend:

  • Advanced degree professionals seeking green card options
  • Researchers and scientists in various fields
  • Entrepreneurs and business professionals
  • Healthcare professionals and physicians
  • Immigration attorneys and paralegals

Key Learning Outcomes:

  • Understand the three-prong test for NIW qualification
  • Learn how to demonstrate "national interest" effectively
  • Master the art of writing persuasive petition letters
  • Identify and gather strong supporting evidence
  • Navigate common RFE scenarios and responses
  • Develop timeline and strategy for your specific case

Course Modules

1
Introduction to EB-2 NIW (30 min)

Overview of the EB-2 category, NIW benefits, and recent USCIS policy updates

2
The Three-Prong Test Explained (45 min)

Deep dive into substantial merit, national importance, and waiver justification

3
Building Your Case Strategy (40 min)

Identifying strengths, addressing weaknesses, and creating compelling narratives

4
Evidence and Documentation (35 min)

Required documents, expert letters, and supporting evidence best practices

5
Petition Preparation and Filing (30 min)

Step-by-step filing process, forms, fees, and timeline expectations

6
Q&A Session (30 min)

Answers to participant questions and case-specific scenarios
